# Email Marketing Campaign - Holiday 2024

## Campaign Overview
Holiday email marketing campaign to drive sales and engagement during the festive season. This campaign includes a series of 8 emails over 6 weeks.

## Campaign Timeline
- November 15: Early bird announcement
- November 22: Black Friday preview
- November 29: Black Friday deals
- December 6: Cyber Monday follow-up
- December 13: Holiday gift guide
- December 20: Last-minute shopping
- December 24: Holiday wishes
- January 2: New year, new beginnings

## Target Audience

### Segment 1: Loyal Customers
- Purchase history: 3+ orders
- Personalized product recommendations
- Exclusive early access to deals
- VIP treatment messaging

### Segment 2: Engaged Subscribers
- Open rate >30%
- Clicked in last 3 months
- Special offers to convert
- Social proof and testimonials

### Segment 3: Inactive Subscribers
- No opens in 6 months
- Re-engagement campaign
- "We miss you" messaging
- Special comeback offer

## Email Content Structure

### Subject Lines
- Keep under 50 characters
- Create urgency and FOMO
- Use emojis sparingly
- A/B test variations

### Email Body
- Compelling hero image
- Clear value proposition
- Single, strong CTA
- Mobile-responsive design
- Personalization tokens

## Design Elements
- Holiday-themed headers
- Brand colors with festive accents
- Clean, scannable layout
- High-quality product images
- Clear hierarchy

## Performance Goals
- Open rate: >25%
- Click-through rate: >4%
- Conversion rate: >2%
- Unsubscribe rate: <0.5%
- Revenue: $100,000+

## Tools and Platforms
- Mailchimp for delivery
- Canva for design
- Google Analytics for tracking
- Hotjar for user behavior

## Testing Strategy
- A/B test subject lines
- Test send times
- Test CTA button colors
- Test email length
- Test personalization levels

## Budget
- Email service: $500/month
- Design resources: $2,000
- Copywriting: $1,500
- Testing tools: $300
Total: $4,300

## Success Metrics
We'll track:
- Delivery rates
- Open rates by segment
- Click-through rates
- Conversion rates
- Revenue per email
- List growth rate
- Engagement over time
