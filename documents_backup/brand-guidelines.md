# Brand Guidelines 2024

## Brand Identity

Our brand represents innovation, trust, and excellence. These guidelines ensure consistent representation across all marketing materials and communications.

## Logo Usage

### Primary Logo
- Use the primary logo whenever possible
- Maintain clear space equal to the height of the logo mark
- Never distort, rotate, or modify the logo
- Minimum size: 40px height for digital, 0.5 inches for print

### Color Variations
- Full color on light backgrounds
- White on dark backgrounds
- Black on light backgrounds when color is not available

## Color Palette

### Primary Colors
- Brand Purple: #667eea
- Brand Violet: #764ba2
- Deep Blue: #2c3e50

### Secondary Colors
- Success Green: #2ecc71
- Warning Orange: #f39c12
- Error Red: #e74c3c
- Info Blue: #3498db

### Neutral Colors
- Dark Gray: #2c3e50
- Medium Gray: #7f8c8d
- Light Gray: #ecf0f1
- White: #ffffff

## Typography

### Headings
- Font: Inter Bold
- Sizes: 48px (H1), 36px (H2), 24px (H3)
- Line height: 1.2

### Body Text
- Font: Inter Regular
- Size: 16px
- Line height: 1.6
- Color: #2c3e50

### Captions
- Font: Inter Medium
- Size: 14px
- Color: #7f8c8d

## Photography Style

### Characteristics
- Bright and vibrant
- Natural lighting preferred
- People-focused when applicable
- Authentic and candid moments

### Composition
- Rule of thirds
- Clear focal point
- Minimal clutter
- Professional quality

## Voice and Tone

### Brand Voice
- Professional yet approachable
- Clear and concise
- Confident but not arrogant
- Helpful and educational

### Tone Variations
- Social Media: Friendly, engaging, conversational
- Email Marketing: Informative, personalized
- Press Releases: Professional, factual
- Product Descriptions: Benefit-focused, clear

## Don'ts
- Never use outdated logo versions
- Never create gradients outside the approved palette
- Never use Comic Sans or similar informal fonts
- Never crop faces awkwardly in photos
- Never use jargon without explanation
