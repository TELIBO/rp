# Social Media Campaign Q4 2024

## Overview
This document outlines our comprehensive social media strategy for Q4 2024, focusing on holiday season engagement across all major platforms including Facebook, Instagram, LinkedIn, and Twitter.

## Campaign Goals
- Increase engagement by 40%
- Grow follower base by 25%
- Drive 50% more traffic to our website
- Boost holiday sales through social channels

## Platform Strategy

### Instagram
- Daily posts featuring product highlights
- Instagram Stories with behind-the-scenes content
- Reels showcasing product demonstrations
- User-generated content campaigns with hashtags #HolidayWithUs

### Facebook
- Community engagement posts
- Facebook Live sessions every Thursday
- Holiday gift guides
- Customer testimonials and reviews

### LinkedIn
- Thought leadership articles
- Company culture highlights
- Industry insights and trends
- Employee spotlight features

### Twitter
- Real-time engagement during trending events
- Quick tips and product highlights
- Customer service responses
- Holiday countdown campaign

## Content Calendar
- Week 1: Brand awareness
- Week 2-3: Product launches
- Week 4-6: Holiday promotions
- Week 7-8: Year-end recap

## KPIs to Track
- Engagement rate
- Reach and impressions
- Click-through rates
- Conversion rates
- Follower growth

## Budget Allocation
- Content creation: $15,000
- Paid advertising: $25,000
- Influencer partnerships: $10,000
- Tools and software: $5,000

Total Budget: $55,000
